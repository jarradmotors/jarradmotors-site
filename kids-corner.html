Kids Corner

Waiting with kids? Here are a few simple car-themed activities to help pass the time while your vehicle is in the workshop.

Parent note

Please keep children in the office and waiting area and away from workshop doors, tools, vehicles and equipment. Workshops are interesting places, but they are not play areas.

Waiting customers are welcome to ask about a free cappuccino.

Office Spotto

From the office or waiting area, can you spot these things?

  • Something red
  • A clock
  • A chair
  • A phone
  • A window
  • A pen
  • A logo
  • A sign
  • A computer
  • A piece of paper
  • Something round
  • Something with numbers on it

Mini Road Safety Quiz

1. What colour is a stop sign?

Answer: Red.

2. What should you wear every car trip?

Answer: A seatbelt.

3. Should you run near parked cars?

Answer: No. Walk with an adult.

4. What do brake lights tell drivers behind you?

Answer: The car is slowing down or stopping.
