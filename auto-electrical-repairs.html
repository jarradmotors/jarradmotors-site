Real Electrical Faults We’ve Fixed

Holden Colorado – No Start & Indicator Fault

Problem: Vehicle would not start and right-hand indicator stuck on.

Diagnosis: Airbag module offline fault traced to missing airbag fuse. Further faults caused by dislodged seat airbag connector. Indicator fault traced to failed stalk.

Fix: Reinstalled fuse, repaired connection and replaced faulty indicator stalk.

Ford Ranger – Park Assist Warning

Problem: Park assist warning constantly on dash.

Diagnosis: Wiring damage found under bull bar causing signal faults.

Fix: Repaired wiring and restored normal system operation.

Mazda 3 – Intermittent Reverse Lights

Problem: Reverse lights working intermittently.

Diagnosis: Fault traced to poor solder joint from aftermarket reverse camera installation.

Fix: Repaired wiring connection and restored consistent operation.
